About Us

Our Mission

An independent investment arm of [Service Credit Union](https://servicecu.org), Service Ventures was founded in 2024 to allow the credit union to keep an eye on the future and further drive its mission of improving members’ financial well-being while also helping improve member experience.

An industry leader in innovation, Service Ventures seeks to partner with startups in the credit union space who are likewise dedicated to improving the financial health of credit union members. If you’re a fintech with a mission to help others, let’s talk.
