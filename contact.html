Frequently Asked Questions

What stage companies do you invest in?

We invest primarily in seed and Series A rounds, with initial investments typically between $1-5 million. We also reserve capital for follow-on investments in our portfolio companies.

What industries do you focus on?

We focus on technology companies across SaaS, fintech, AI/ML, and health tech verticals. We're particularly interested in companies leveraging technology to transform traditional industries.

What should I include in my pitch?

A good pitch includes your team background, the problem you're solving, your solution and technology, market size, competitive landscape, traction to date, and your funding needs and plans for using the capital.

How long does your investment process take?

Our investment process typically takes 4-6 weeks from initial meeting to funding decision. For companies with urgent capital needs, we can sometimes accelerate this timeline.
